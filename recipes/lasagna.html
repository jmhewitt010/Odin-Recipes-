Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a rich and decadent Italian dish made with soft flat noodles and a hearty meat sauce and ricotta cheese. These are layered to perfection and then topped with lucious mozzarella cheese and then baked to perfection.

Ingredients

Cheese Mixture

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese (2 cups)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna Noodles / Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles, plus extra in case of breakage
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  • Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients.
  • Heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes.
  • Add the ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high. Use a spatula to break up the meat so that it’s very fine and crumbled. Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through. Add garlic during the last minute. Drain excess grease.
  • Add the chicken broth and use a silicone spatula to “clean” the bottom and sides of the pot. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently while you boil the pasta noodles.

Preheat Oven and Cook the Noodles

  • Preheat oven to 375°.
  • Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook noodles to al dente according to package instructions.
  • Stir gently to prevent sticking. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  • Lay the noodles flat on wax or parchment paper to cool while you assemble the lasagna.

Assemble

  • Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
  • Add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping if needed.
  • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, followed by about 1½ cups of meat sauce.
  • Top with 4 more noodles, another 1/3 of ricotta, and more sauce.
  • Finish with 4 noodles, remaining ricotta, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  • Top with 2½ cups of mozzarella cheese.
  • Spray foil with nonstick spray and place spray-side down on the baking dish.

Bake

  • Bake covered for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake an additional 10 minutes.
  • Remove from oven and garnish with fresh parsley.
  • Let sit for 15 minutes before serving to set the layers.
  • Serve with garlic bread and cheese.
